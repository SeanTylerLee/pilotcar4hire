About us

Free for everyone. Seriously.

We built Pilot Car 4 Hire to help pilot cars get found and help carriers get loads moved — without fees, paywalls, or middlemen.

Our mission

Get pilot cars listed. Get carriers connected. Charge nobody.

This directory exists for the greater good of the pilot car and heavy haul community. We’re not here to squeeze anyone for a subscription or a lead fee. We want more drivers visible, more carriers finding escorts, and more oversize freight moving safely across the country.

FREE

Pilot cars list for free

Create your listing, show up on the map, and let carriers find you — no listing fees, no monthly charges, no “premium” tier. Your business name, services, states, and contact info are out there at no cost.

FREE

Carriers browse for free

Search by state, filter by service, view listings, and call or email pilot cars directly. No account required to browse. No credits to buy. No gatekeeping between you and the driver you need.

100%

Direct contact

We don’t broker your calls or take a cut. When a carrier finds a pilot car on our site, they reach out to you directly. Simple, honest, and how this industry ought to work.

Why we built this

Pilot cars keep oversize and heavy haul freight safe on the road. Carriers need them fast when a load is ready. Too often, getting visible online means paying for ads, lead services, or platforms that weren’t built with drivers in mind.

We think a free pilot car directory should just work: drivers get listed, carriers get contact info, and everyone saves time and money. That’s the whole point.

Whether you’re a one-truck operation or a fleet of escort vehicles, you deserve a place on the map without opening your wallet. Whether you’re dispatching one load or running lanes every week, you deserve to find certified pilot cars without a paywall.

What we don’t do

  • We don’t charge pilot cars to be listed
  • We don’t charge carriers to search or contact drivers
  • We don’t sell your leads or hide listings behind upgrades
  • We don’t get in the middle of your phone calls and emails

Ready to use it?

It’s free. Always has been. That’s the plan.
